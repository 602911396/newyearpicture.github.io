 The new year picture may be one of most popular artworks in Chinese folk. Most places all have the custom to post the new lunar new year's painting on new year’s day， Because of  the lunar new year's season replaces ， After or posts may enjoy one year ， Therefore is called ： "New year picture". As it proved ， primarily lunar new year's painting is from the faith of exorcises evil spirits, which that  Watches and increases the festive atmosphere spirits. From the Zhou Dynasty ， Every time meets festival ， The people then will use the peach plank to mark two gods pictures ， Hangs two leans ， to the front door or the bedroom; Uses to expel evil spirits ， Prays Falkner to be auspicious. Tang Dynasty, it also has been appeared the pictures of qiong Qin and chigong Wei, Who can drive evils. Later the gate gods are shown more and more.

 The wood engraving lunar new year's painting appears in Tang ， Popularizes in Song. After Qian long-Jia qing times in Qing Dynasty, it gradually appears the place school and the collection sells habitat ，The famous three big Xylograph lunar new year's painting habitat is Suzhou’s taohuawu， Tianjin’s Yangliuqing, Weifang's Yangjiabu.

The Weifang’s Yangjiabu Xylograph lunar new year's painting initiates at the end of Ming Dynasty， Prosper in Qian Long years. Xylograph lunar new year's painting of Yangjiabu，is one rare and beautiful flower in our country folk art house，But are renowned at home and abroad by his styles of  the rich local breath and the pure bright.
