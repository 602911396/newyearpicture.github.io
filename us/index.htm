Xylograph is one rare and beautiful flower in art fields in Chinese traditional folk. In China, it has three origins, which are Tianjin’s yangliuqing, Suzhou’s taohuawu and Weifang’s yangjiabu. New year’s painting of yangjiabu in Weifang is famous of lively and unpretending, is called of one treasure in Chinese traditional art.

Xylograph of yangjiabu is originate in the end of Song Dynasty, and is matured in Ming Dynasty. Develops today, it has long history. In several hundred years evolution development, it Not only has created thousands artistic valuable thing, but also has accomplished the multitudinous folk artists.

Mr. Luoshu Yang is representative personage in modern art and is also heritor of famous painting store “tong shun de”. When he was very young, he studied to make painting. Through his constant efforts, he specializes in painting, carving, printing and in the round. From now on, his feat is gradually perfect and more comprehensive. Especially on carving. He create his own method courses, is called “lifelike and transfixion”. His writing is called rattler of yangjiabu xylograph.

To keep exploiting new subjects, Mr. Yang is inebriety in national life. He assimilates from original works, and plays exhuming, collecting and adapting. He wrote many excellent works on the basis of that. For example, <regnal picture past dynasties> is unmatched painting production. the calendar he made is the first one in China. In addition, <one hundred and eight heroes in mountain Liang> is another classic. For finished this work, he went to original place in person for many times. It cost three years’ time and more twenty-five thousand Yuan to finish it. It shows lifelike every detail. Everyone have to say : “ it’s a rare wonder in modern!”

Mr. Luoshu Yang is selfless, and he is well known in oversea. He had played his feat to visitors in the world. The Cambodian King and Queen have ever visited his play.  Moreover, our national leader, ruihuan lee, has also visited him in person. After sighting his act, he had a high recognition. The most estimable, he took his collection to China History Museum in 1993.it has proved that Xylograph he present is the earliest works so far.

Today, Mr. Yang present <a hundred and eight heroes in Mountain Liang> to the museum as a expensive gift, he take this opportunity to express his love and cordial for his art.

At last, we wish Mr. Luoshu Yang keeping green and making more and more excellent works in future.

From vice president of weifang city   Jinlan Zheng
